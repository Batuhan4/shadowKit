The Solution
ShadowKit lets a DAO vote in the shadows and execute in the light.
  • Members vote with zero-knowledge proofs — who voted, how much weight they hold, and which way they voted all stay private.
  • The running tally is timelock-sealed until close, so whales can’t watch the result and swing it at the last second.
  • An LLM-bounded AI agent then executes the approved action on-chain — inside a policy it mathematically cannot break.
Think
It’s infrastructure, not a one-off — an SDK + Soroban contracts any Stellar DAO drops in for private voting and bounded agent execution.

PMF
Web3 governance is broken in two ways at once.
  • Public votes invite manipulation. Whales and analytics firms watch the live tally, vote last, and swing outcomes — while small holders self-censor.
  • “Let an AI run the treasury” is terrifying. A hallucinating agent with signing power can move real funds, so DAOs stay stuck with slow, manual multisig.
  • DAO treasuries hold billions on-chain, yet their decisions are neither truly private nor safely autonomous.
Think
Why should anyone care? Because today you must pick private or autonomous. Every DAO managing real money feels this — ShadowKit gives both.

Technical Workflow
One sealed-vote-to-execution loop — every step live on Stellar testnet.

Vote

snarkjs builds a real Groth16 proof in your browser (BLS12-381). Identity, weight & direction stay hidden.

Seal

Each vote is timelock-encrypted to a future drand round. On-chain, the tally reads back null.

Reveal

At the deadline the drand beacon drops; close_and_reveal aggregates the weighted tally on-chain → Approved.

Execute

Agent pays for data over x402 → Gemini plans → on-chain AgentPolicy allows only the capped, approved swap → Executed.
